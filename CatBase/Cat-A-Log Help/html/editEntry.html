Editing an entry in the show

Entries in a show can be edited.

Click the button, or type (enter), or double click an entry, to start editing the highlighted cat.

This will bring up the edit sheet.
Select whatever details you wish to change,
and type in the new details.
Type (tab key) after entering information into a box, to ensure it is entered.

After changing all the details that you wish,
Click or type (enter) to accept the editing,

Click or type (esc) to cancel the editing.

The cat is now edited.
