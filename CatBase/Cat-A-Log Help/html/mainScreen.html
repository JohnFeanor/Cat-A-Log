The Main Screen



The main window is split into two parts.

Show Section

A list of shows in the database is given here.

To add a new show to the database, click the '+' button

When a new show is added, the show data sheet becomes available

To delete the currently selected show, click the '-' button The currently selected show will be deleted. There is no undo for this.
    The show data sheet appears.
    Enter:
    • the name of the show.
    • the number of rings in the show (from 1 to 6)
    • the date of the show
    • the affiliation of the show
    • the initials of the judges for the rings

    QFA Show divides the show into Longhair, ShortHair and Companion.
    ACF Show divides the show into Group 1, Group 2, Group 3 and Group 4.
    COAWA Show divides the show into Persian Type, Semi Longhair, Siamese Type, Other Short Hair Type, Other Foreign Shorthair and Companion.

    Click or press return to accept the new show.
    Click or press esc to reject the new show.
   

 
Clicking the edit button, or just pressing the return key, will bring up a cat entry sheet allowing you to change the details of the selected cat.
Clicking the add cat button, or just pressing the +/= key, will bring up a cat entry sheet allowing you to enter a new cat into the show.
Clicking the remove button, or pressing the delete key will delete the selected cat from the show, but leave it in the database. You will be asked to confirm the deletion.





The cats will always be exported into a doc file in show order.
